MY SKILLS

Adaptability

Being open to change and able to adjust to new situations or environments.

Quickly acquiring new skills or knowledge when faced with unfamiliar challenges.

Embracing uncertainty: Thriving in uncertain or ambiguous situations and making the best of them.

Creativity

Thinking outside the box to develop original ideas or solutions.

Artistic expression: Using visual arts, music, writing, or other forms to communicate ideas and emotions.

Design thinking: Applying creative problem-solving methods to design products, processes, or systems.

Self-Motivation

Taking action without needing direction or motivation from others.

Setting personal goals and taking steps to achieve them.

Continuing to work toward a goal despite obstacles or setbacks.

Emotional Resilience

Being kind to yourself in times of struggle or failure.

Maintaining a positive outlook even when facing difficult situations.

Controlling emotional reactions to maintain balance and well-being during challenging moments

Self-Sufficiency

Learning basic life skills like cooking, cleaning, and home repairs.

Knowledge of basic first aid, navigation, and how to handle emergency situations.

Being able to manage your own finances without relying on others.
